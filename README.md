# International Business Plan – Estonia

This repository presents an international business plan developed for a startup expansion into the Estonian market.

## Project Overview
The document covers key aspects of international business development, including:
- Company description and justification
- Market and industry research
- Supplier and commercialization strategy
- Exporting model
- Growth strategy
- Organizational and administrative structure
- Financial and investment considerations

## Skills Demonstrated
- International trade and market entry analysis  
- Business and financial planning  
- Strategic thinking and structured documentation  
- Commercial and export-oriented mindset  

## Context
This project was developed as an academic–professional exercise to demonstrate applied knowledge in international business and global market expansion.
